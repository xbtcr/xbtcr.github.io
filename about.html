About XBTCR

XBTCR is a community-driven token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) with a focus on transparency, fairness, and long-term growth. Our mission is to create a decentralized token that rewards holders while providing a sustainable and trustless trading environment.

Our Vision

To make XBTCR a recognizable digital asset across decentralized exchanges and crypto platforms worldwide. We aim to provide utility through community tools, apps, and innovative DeFi features.

What Makes XBTCR Unique?

  • 🔥 99% Supply Burned – Only 1 Trillion in circulation
  • 💰 Low Tax Structure – Just 3.5% (2% Marketing + 1.5% Auto-Burn)
  • 🔓 100% Open Trading – No whitelist or blacklist
  • 🌍 Growing Ecosystem – App, Tools, and Community Building

Our Commitment

We are committed to full transparency, community engagement, and continuous improvements. The XBTCR team is dedicated to delivering value and building trust with every step.
