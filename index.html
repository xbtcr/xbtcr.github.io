About XBTCR

XBTCR is a revolutionary decentralized token built on Binance Smart Chain, designed to bring innovation and transparency to the DeFi space. Our mission is to create a fair and accessible financial ecosystem for everyone.

With a total supply of 100 billion tokens, XBTCR implements a unique burn mechanism that ensures long-term value appreciation for holders. The project is completely community-driven with no central authority.

Key features include:

  • Secure and transparent transactions
  • Low transaction fees (3.5% total tax)
  • Automatic liquidity generation
  • Regular token burns to reduce supply
  • Community governance model
