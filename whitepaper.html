XBTCR Whitepaper

The complete technical documentation of the XBTCR ecosystem and token

Abstract

XBTCR is a revolutionary decentralized token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) that aims to bridge the gap between traditional finance and decentralized finance (DeFi). Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem that rewards holders while providing real utility in the cryptocurrency space.

This whitepaper outlines the technical specifications, economic model, and roadmap for the XBTCR project. It serves as the definitive guide to understanding the vision, technology, and implementation of the XBTCR ecosystem.

Technical Specifications

Blockchain Network

XBTCR is implemented as a BEP-20 token on the Binance Smart Chain, chosen for its low transaction fees, high throughput, and compatibility with the Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM).

Token Details

  • Token Name: XBTCR
  • Token Symbol: XBTCR
  • Decimals: 18
  • Total Supply: 100 Trillion
  • Circulating Supply: 1 Trillion
  • Burned Tokens: 99 Trillion
  • Contract Address: 0x4274c09719F4eb538682F74DB27f7DB6DE330cd9

Smart Contract Features

The XBTCR smart contract includes the following key features:

  • Auto-burning mechanism (1.5% of each transaction)
  • Marketing tax (2% of each transaction)
  • No blacklist or whitelist functions (fully decentralized)
  • Liquidity pool integration

Economic Model

Token Distribution

The XBTCR token distribution is designed to ensure long-term sustainability and growth of the ecosystem:

  • Public Sale: 40% of total supply
  • Team & Advisors: 15% (vested over 24 months)
  • Ecosystem Development: 25%
  • Marketing & Liquidity: 12%
  • Reserve Fund: 8%

Transaction Taxes

Each transaction is subject to the following taxes:

  • Marketing Tax: 2% - Funds marketing and development
  • Auto-Burn Tax: 1.5% - Permanently removes tokens from circulation
  • Total Tax: 3.5%

Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation (Q1-Q2 2025)

  • Token launch on PancakeSwap
  • Initial marketing campaigns
  • Community building

Phase 2: Growth (Q3-Q4 2025)

  • CEX listings
  • Staking platform launch
  • Partnership announcements

Phase 3: Expansion (2026)

  • Ecosystem product launches
  • Cross-chain integration
  • Full decentralization

Conclusion

XBTCR represents a new paradigm in decentralized finance, combining innovative tokenomics with real utility. Our vision is to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that rewards long-term holders while continuously reducing supply through our burn mechanism.

We invite you to join us on this journey as we build the future of finance together.
