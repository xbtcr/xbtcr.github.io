General Questions

What is XBTCR?

XBTCR is a decentralized cryptocurrency token built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) that combines innovative tokenomics with real utility. Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem that rewards holders while providing valuable services in the DeFi space.

The token features automatic liquidity generation, static rewards for holders, and a dedicated development fund to ensure continuous improvement of the ecosystem.

How do I buy XBTCR?

You can purchase XBTCR on PancakeSwap, the leading decentralized exchange on Binance Smart Chain. Follow these steps:

  • Set up a compatible wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet
  • Fund your wallet with BNB (Binance Coin)
  • Go to PancakeSwap and connect your wallet
  • Swap BNB for XBTCR using our contract address: 0x4274c09719F4eb538682F74DB27f7DB6DE330cd9

For a detailed guide, check our step-by-step tutorial.

Is XBTCR safe?

Security is our top priority. XBTCR has implemented multiple safety measures:

  • Smart contract audited by a reputable third-party firm
  • Liquidity locked for 5 years
  • Renounced ownership to prevent malicious changes
  • No mint function to prevent inflation

You can view our complete audit report on the Audit page.

Tokenomics & Rewards

How do the rewards work?

XBTCR implements a 10% transaction fee that is distributed as follows:

  • 5% redistributed to all holders (static rewards)
  • 3% added to liquidity pool automatically
  • 2% sent to development/marketing wallet

This means you earn more XBTCR just by holding tokens in your wallet. The more transactions occur in the network, the more rewards you accumulate automatically.

When will I receive my rewards?

Rewards are distributed in real-time with each transaction across the network. There's no need to claim them - they're automatically added to your wallet balance.

You can track your increasing balance by viewing your wallet on BscScan or in your wallet app. The amount you receive is proportional to your percentage of the total supply.

What is the total supply of XBTCR?

The total supply of XBTCR is 1 quadrillion (1,000,000,000,000,000) tokens. However, over 50% of this supply was burned at launch, and additional burns occur with each transaction through our deflationary mechanism.

The circulating supply decreases over time, creating scarcity and potential value appreciation for holders.

Technical Questions

Which wallets support XBTCR?

XBTCR can be stored in any wallet that supports Binance Smart Chain tokens, including:

  • MetaMask
  • Trust Wallet
  • SafePal
  • Math Wallet
  • TokenPocket

Remember to add XBTCR as a custom token using our contract address: 0x4274c09719F4eb538682F74DB27f7DB6DE330cd9

Why are my tokens not showing in my wallet?

If your XBTCR tokens aren't visible, you likely need to add the token manually to your wallet:

  1. Get the XBTCR contract address: 0x4274c09719F4eb538682F74DB27f7DB6DE330cd9
  2. In your wallet, find the option to "Add Custom Token"
  3. Select Binance Smart Chain network
  4. Enter the contract address (token symbol and decimals should auto-populate)
  5. Save and your balance should appear

If you still have issues, check the transaction on BscScan to confirm it was successful.
