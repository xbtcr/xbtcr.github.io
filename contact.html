Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase XBTCR tokens?

You can purchase XBTCR tokens on PancakeSwap. Connect your wallet to the platform, ensure you have BNB for gas fees, and swap BNB for XBTCR using our contract address: 0x4274c09719F4eb538682F74DB27f7DB6DE330cd9. We recommend setting slippage to 2%.

What wallets support XBTCR?

XBTCR is a BEP-20 token compatible with any wallet that supports Binance Smart Chain, including MetaMask, Trust Wallet, and Binance Chain Wallet. Make sure you're connected to the Binance Smart Chain network in your wallet.

How can I stake my XBTCR tokens?

Our staking platform will be launched in Q2 2025. Once live, you'll be able to stake your XBTCR tokens directly through our website to earn rewards. Stay tuned for announcements!

What is the tokenomics of XBTCR?

XBTCR has a total supply of 100 trillion tokens, with 99 trillion burned. Each transaction has a 3.5% tax (2% for marketing and 1.5% auto-burned). You can find complete details in our Tokenomics section.

How can I join the XBTCR community?

We have active communities on Telegram, Twitter, and Discord. Follow the social media links on this page to join our growing community of XBTCR enthusiasts!
